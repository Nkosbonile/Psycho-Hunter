About Psycho Hunter

Psycho Hunter pulls you into the depths of an abandoned mansion where every creak, every shadow conceals a secret, and every decision takes you deeper into the unknown. Navigate dark hallways, uncover twisted clues, and confront the horrors that lie within. Will you uncover the truth, or will the house claim you as its own?

Game Features

  • Immersive horror atmosphere
  • Challenging puzzles and levels
  • Interactive storyline with branching paths
  • Dark, mysterious characters
